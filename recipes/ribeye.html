Ribeye Steak At Home

Description

Learn how to cook Ribeye Steak perfectly like they do in restaurants. This recipe will teach you how you can do it from home.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak 8-10 oz sliced 1-1.5 inch thick
  • butter 2 oz
  • Fresh Rosemary
  • Fresh Thyme
  • 2-3 peeled garlic cloves
  • Avocado/Olive oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. We need to get our meat to room temperature first. Take your meat out of the fridge for about 30-45 minutes.
  2. Once the ribeye is room temperature, pat dry with a paper towel. Season with salt & pepper.We need to heat up our skillet(iron cast skillet preferred) to the point that is smoking to sear the surface of the steak. We are going to cook the steak for a total of 5 minutes(medium rare to medium). Cook it or under cook it a minute longer depends on how you want your steak cooked.
  3. Once hot enough, put enough cooking oil to cover the skillet. We are going to sear each side of the steak for about a minute. (note: keep flipping each side for a minute to cook it evenly on both sides)
  4. Lower the heat to medium and put the butter,garlic,rosemary, thyme into the skillet. Let the butter melt and baste the steak with the sauce. Remember to keep flipping.
  5. After a total of 5 minutes or however long you cooked it, let the steak rest outside of the heat and let sit for 10 minutes.
  6. Slice meat along the grain for a more chewable texture and Enjoy your perfectly cooked steak.
