Salt & Pepper Wings

Description

Chinese-style chicken wings that has a little bit of a KICK!!!!Chicken Wings combined with spicy peppers and szechuan salt seasoning to get a simple but mild spicy flavor profile.

Ingredients

  • Shaoxing Wine
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Chicken Wings
  • Potato Starch
  • Avocado Oil
  • fresno & jalapeno peppers
  • Ground White pepper
  • Szechuan Pepper Salt

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken (15 to 20 minutes) in a mixture of rice wine, garlic, and ginger to give it lots of flavor.
  2. Coat the wings in starch
  3. Cook the wings in the pre-heated cooking oil (350 F) for about 8-10 minutes. Set them aside once cook.
  4. Cut the chili peppers and garlic. Stir fry them quickly in avocado oil
  5. Toss the cooked wings into the stir-fried Ingredients and season with the pepper salt and white peppers
